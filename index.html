He Tweets, We Give

When he tweets about Islam, I will give $10 to the Council on American-Islamic Relations.
When he tweets about stolen elections, I will give $10 to the NAACP.
When he tweets about illegal immigration, I will give $10 to Border Angels.
When he tweets about the biased media, I will give $10 to the Committee To Protect Journalists.
When he tweets about being pro-life, I will give $10 to Planned Parenthood.
When he tweets about climate change, I will give $10 to Earth Justice.
When he tweets about ratings, I will give $10 to the Malala Fund.
When I give, I will include a 2% tip to support this project.
